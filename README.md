## 👥 **WHO WE ARE**

We are **Fourgay**—a fun and spirited group of students from the **University of Transport and Communications in Ho Chi Minh City**. Originally a team of **4 guys**, our name started as a lighthearted joke. Now, with one member having moved on, we continue our journey as a team of three, proudly carrying forward the name as a symbol of our bond and humor.
